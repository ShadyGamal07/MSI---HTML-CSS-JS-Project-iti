Company Brief

MSI is a world leader in gaming, content creation, business & productivity and AIoT solutions. Bolstered by its

cutting-edge R&D capabilities and customer-driven innovation, MSI has a wide-ranging global presence

spanning over 120 countries. Its comprehensive lineup of laptops, graphics cards, monitors, motherboards,

desktops, peripherals, servers, IPCs, robotic appliances, and vehicle infotainment and telematics systems are

globally acclaimed. Committed to advancing user experiences through the finest product quality, intuitive user

interface and design aesthetics, MSI is a leading brand that shapes the future of technology.
